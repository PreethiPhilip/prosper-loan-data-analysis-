Variables For Predicting the Borrower Rate¶

by (Preethi Philip)¶

Investigation Overview¶

For the presentation, I just focus on features that could affect the borrower rate, which are the original Loan Amount, Prosper rating and Loan Status. I started by showing the distribution of borrower rate and Loan Status.Then, I showed the relationship between Prosper rate vs Borrower Rate,Borrower rate vs Loan Status,Borrower rate vs Loan Amount as well as prosper rating vs other numerical variables such as StatedMonthlyIncome','MonthlyLoanPayment','LoanOriginalAmount','CreditScoreRangeLower'.

Dataset Overview¶

The Original data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others. The Original Dataset contains so many variables.But I have selected few features for my analysis. 'Term','LoanStatus','LoanOriginalAmount','BorrowerRate','ProsperRating(Alpha)','ProsperScore','DebtToIncomeRatio',EmploymentStatus', 'StatedMonthlyIncome','Occupation','LoanOriginationDate','MonthlyLoanPayment','CreditScoreRangeLower,

Distribution of BorrowerRate¶

This is the Borrower's interest rate for this loan.The Borrower rate is between 0.1 to 0.2.The distribution of Borrower rate is looks multimodal. A small peak is at 0.1, a large peak centered at 0.15. There is also a very sharpe peak between 0.3 and 0.35.

Distribution of Loan Status¶

The current status of the loan: Cancelled, Chargedoff, Completed, Current, Defaulted, FinalPaymentInProgress, PastDue. The PastDue status will be accompanied by a delinquency bucket.Most of the loans are current loan. And the other big part is the Completed loan.The number of past due and cancelled are quite low.

Loan Status vs BorrowerRate¶

The heighest Borrower rate is for currently active loan status.

ProsperScore vs Borrower Rate¶

The Plot shows that there is a strong relation between borrower rate and Prosper score, Higher the Prosperscore, lower the Borrower rate

LoanOriginalAmount vs Borrower Rate¶

The borrower's rate is negatively related to the loan amount.Borrower Rate limit decreases as the loan amount increases.

ProsperScore vs Numerical Variables¶

There is a strong relation between borrower rate Prosper score and CreditScore.Higher the Prosperscore, lower the Borrower rate.Higher the CreditScore, lower the Borrower rate. Monthly loan payment and Prosper score has a positive correlation. Monthly loan payment and loan original amount has also a strong correlation. Borrowers who have the highest credit score and prosper score will have the lowest interest rates. The higher the loan amount, the lower the borrower's rate.
